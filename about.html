About Space Builder

Space Builder is a solar system simulation web-app, where users can create stars and planets and assemble them into solar systems. Users will submit basic information about their astronomical objects, and the application calculates additional detailed characteristics. Each planet will be visualized on a plot centered around the parent star, showing its distance and orbital path. For each planet in a system, the application will give a 'habitability score,' ranking each planet by its likelihood to support life. Space Builder is not only educational, offering insights into various celestial bodies and the conditions for life, but also fun, as users watch their own systems come together, piece-by-piece.
